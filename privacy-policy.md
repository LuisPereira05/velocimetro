## English

# Privacy Policy for LM - HUD Speedometer

**Last updated:** November 30, 2025

## Overview
LM - HUD Speedometer is a speedometer app that displays your current speed using your device's GPS.

## Data Collection and Usage
- **Location Data**: We access your device's GPS location solely to calculate and display your current speed in real-time.
- **No Storage**: We do NOT store your location data anywhere.
- **No Transmission**: We do NOT send your location data to any servers or third parties.
- **Local Processing**: All speed calculations happen locally on your device.

## Permissions
The app requires location permission to function. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings, though the app will not work without it.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
